The plan covers all 6 requested sections. A few decisions worth flagging:

**Gauge:** Modeled as an inline SVG speedometer — gradient arc (red→green→red), needle pivoting from the arc center, pendulum driven by a power-curved sine so it lingers at the red extremes and sweeps fast through the green center. Depth mapping makes the center = maxDepth, edges = maxDepth−2m, which matches the PRD's "min depth = max depth−2m" rule.

**Catch input:** `pointerdown` teleports the hook X instantly (no lerp) — that's intentional. Lerp only kicks in on `pointermove`. This is the difference between drag feeling responsive vs. sluggish.

**Rarity juice:** Legendary is dramatically different — 1.9× fish size, pitch-shifted audio at 1.8×, 10px screen shake, full-screen white flash, 50 coin particles + 16 canvas stars. Common catch is intentionally understated so the contrast lands.

**Audio strategy:** `rob_use.mp3` does double duty — upgrade purchase (normal pitch) and hook launch (pitch 1.2, louder). This matches the 3-file constraint while keeping the launch feeling punchy.
